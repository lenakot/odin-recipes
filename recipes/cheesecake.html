Cheesecake

Description

This creamy cheesecake is great with a variety of toppings - or even by itself!

Ingredients
  • 1.5 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 0.5 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 0.5 cup margarine
  • 3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
  • 1.5 cups white sugar\
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 0.25 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups sour cream
Steps

Step 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, 3 tablespoons sugar, cinnamon and melted margarine. Mix all ingredients well and press into bottom and sides of a 9 inch springform pan.

Step 2. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Blend in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla and salt. Fold in the sour cream.

Step 3. Pour batter into springform pan. Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour. Turn off oven and leave cake in for at least 30 minutes.
