Baked French Fries

Description

This baked French fries recipe is an easy way to make a great side dish for burgers!

Ingredients

  • 1 large baking potato
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 0.5 teaspoon paprika
  • 0.5 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon chili powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon onion powder

Steps

Step 1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).

Step 2. Cut potato into thick wedges. Mix olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, chili powder, and onion powder together in a medium bowl. Add potato wedges and toss to coat evenly; arrange on a baking sheet.

Step 3. Bake in the preheated oven, turning once or twice, until golden brown and crispy, about 45 minutes.
